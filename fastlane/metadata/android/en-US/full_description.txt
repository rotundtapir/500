500 is a classic 4-player partnership trick-taking card game, popular in
Australia and New Zealand. Partner up and play against three computer opponents.

Features:
* Standard 4-player Australian rules: a 43-card deck with the Joker, a 3-card
  kitty, bidding from 6 to 10 tricks across the suits and no-trumps, plus Misère
  and Open Misère.
* Play offline against three AI opponents — no account, no network required.
* First partnership to 500 points wins.

This app is free and open source. The version on F-Droid and GitHub is completely
ad-free; if you enjoy it, please consider supporting development via the donation
link. (The Google Play version instead shows ads, with an optional purchase to
remove them.)
